WHO WE ARE

More than just a digitizing service — we're embroiderers too

Puncher.com – We digitize it right, so your embroidery shines bright.

We are a digitizing company that serves embroiderers worldwide who need to transform images into embroidery files for computerized embroidery machines. Our mission is to provide top quality digitizing, with a fast turnaround at a fair price.

Since 1993, when we opened our first embroidery shop, we've learned how to satisfy even the most demanding customers. We operate four embroidery shops, which puts us ahead of competitors who only digitize but never actually run an embroidery machine. Our daily experience at our shops guarantees the quality and integrity of our work.

Digitizing is the heart of embroidery — choose your digitizer wisely. It will reflect your future in the embroidery business. We have satisfied customers in USA, Canada, UK, Germany, Switzerland, Netherlands, Sweden, Norway, Denmark and many other countries around the world.

OUR SERVICES

Professional digitizing and vector services for every need

🎯 Embroidery Digitizing

Custom digitizing for logos, lettering, and complex artwork. Optimized for left chest, caps, jacket backs, and more. All stitch types including satin, fill, and running stitch.

🎨 Vector Art Conversion

Convert JPG, PNG, BMP images to scalable vector formats (AI, EPS, CDR). Perfect for screen printing, vinyl cutting, and high-resolution printing.

📐 Editing & Resizing

Modify existing designs, resize for different applications, adjust stitch density, and convert between file formats. Quick turnaround on all edits.

🔄 Format Conversion

Convert your files to any format: DST, PES, EMB, EXP, JEF, HUS, SEW, XXX, and more. Compatible with Tajima, Barudan, Brother, Melco, and all major machines.
