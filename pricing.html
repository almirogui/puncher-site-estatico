A note about gradients and photorealism

True color gradients and photographic images cannot be reproduced exactly in embroidery — thread is not ink. Our digitizers will adapt your artwork to achieve the best possible result within the natural limitations of the embroidery medium, and will show you a digital preview before production begins. This is part of our standard process, at no extra charge.

Special Services

Techniques that go beyond standard digitizing

These services require a fundamentally different digitizing approach. A 25% surcharge applies over the standard stitch-count price. Combining two specials (e.g. Rush + 3D Puff) results in a 50% surcharge.

⚡

Rush Delivery

+25% surcharge

Your order jumps to the front of the queue. Delivered within 4–6 hours, any day of the week. Available for all placements.

🧵

Appliqué Digitizing

+25% surcharge

Appliqué stitches fabric pieces onto the garment. Placement stitches, tack-down layers, and finishing borders are mapped separately — a completely different process.

🏔️

3D Puff Digitizing

+25% surcharge

Creates a raised, three-dimensional effect using foam underneath the stitches. Requires a specially built file — standard files cannot simply be converted.
