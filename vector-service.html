Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer color separation services?

Yes. Our vector art service includes professional color separation for screen printing, DTF transfers, vinyl cutting and sublimation. We deliver AI, EPS and CDR files with properly separated color layers — each color on its own layer, correctly named and ready for your print shop or cutting machine.

How to place an order?

Simply fill out the registration form, then go to another page and fill out the form with the logo attached. You will receive a message confirming that we have started your order.

How long will it take to get my vector back?

Orders are usually shipped within 1 day or less after we receive your order. However, we do have an express service for urgent orders with delivery within a few hours at a special rate.

What time zone are you in?

Brazil is in the same time zone as North America, with similar working hours. This means fast communication and quick turnaround for US customers.

Do your prices include corrections?

Yes, we will make any corrections you need. Additional charges will only apply if the original design sent to us is changed.

Do you do vectorization of old or low quality logos?

Yes, we can work with old logos or low-resolution images and turn them into high-quality vector files.
