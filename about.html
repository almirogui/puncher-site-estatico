What is Puncher.com?

Puncher.com is a digitizing company that serves embroiderers worldwide who need to transform images into embroidery files for computerized embroidery machines.

Puncher.com and Magic Cap are two partner companies. Puncher.com specializes in embroidery digitizing while Magic Cap handles the embroidery production. They were founded in March 1993 in the city of Belo Horizonte, Minas Gerais, Brazil, by Almiro Guimarães and D. Bossi.

In over three decades of operation, we have watched the embroidery market grow and evolve — and we've adapted with it. Today, we bring substantial experience to every project. We are specialists in embroidered headwear and sportswear designs.

Our internet presence was established in 1999, and since then we've served thousands of customers around the world. We are known for our high-quality designs and competitive pricing.

What Makes Us Different

Real experience from real embroiderers — not just a digitizing service

🏪 We Run Our Own Shops

Magic Cap operates four embroidery shops. This daily experience with real customers makes the difference between us and other internet digitizers. We know what works on the machine because we run it ourselves.

🌎 Same Timezone Advantage

Brazil is in the same timezone as North America, with similar working hours. This means faster communication, quicker responses, and no overnight delays.

🖥️ Professional Setup

Our digitizing office uses professional software including Wilcom and maintains multiple embroidery machines for testing: Tajima TMFX, Tajima NEO, SWF, and Brother.

📁 Your Designs, Archived

Finished designs belong only to the customer and are archived for 2 years. Need an edit on a design from last year? No problem.
