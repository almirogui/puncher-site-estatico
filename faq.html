Frequently Asked Questions

If you can't find an answer for your question, please contact us.

How do I place an order?

Just fill out the registration form, then go to the order page and fill out the form with logo attached. You will receive a message confirming that we started your order.

How long will it take to get my design?

Orders are typically shipped within 1 day or less after we receive your order. But we have an express service for rush orders with few hours turnaround at a special rate.

Do I have to pay shipping?

Shipping does not apply since it's an emailed file. You receive your digitized designs directly via email.

How can I pay for my order?

Payment can be made by credit card, bank transfer or PayPal. We have a secure page where the customer can complete the payment process.

Do you send invoices?

Yes, we always send an invoice to assist our customers in monitoring their expenses with us.

How do I start using your services?

For first-time customers, we request you to fill out our registration page completely. As soon as the registration is done, it means we started working for you.

Do your prices include corrections?

Yes, we will make all corrections that you need. Additional charges will only apply if the original design sent to us is altered.

How can I send my logo?

There are 3 ways to provide us with your logo:

  • By email: Simply attach any common format (gif, jpg, bmp, etc.) to an email and send us (Send us a message)
  • From the web: If your logo is visible on the web simply email us the URL address
  • By our order form: Use our online order form to upload your logo directly
Can you edit a file made by another company?

No, we can only edit a file made by us.

What if I'm not satisfied with my design?

Let us know! We'll do whatever it takes to satisfy your expectations. Our policy is: we seek for the satisfaction of our customers. No 30-day restrictions and no limited warranties. Our designs and services are guaranteed to live up to your expectations. If you ever have a comment, concern, or complaint, simply contact us and we'll make it right. It's that simple.

What file formats do you provide?

We will send the files in the extension that suits your embroidery machine. We work with DST, EMB, PXF, PES, and many more. Check our File Formats page for a complete list.

Do you work with machine cards?

We don't work with machines cards. We send files attachments that you can transfer to USB Flash drives or send to a machine through your embroidery software.

Can you send files in outline/vector format?

Yes, we send it in file extension .EMB (this is the native format of the software Wilcom) or .PXF (Tajima/Pulse).
